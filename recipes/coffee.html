Coffee

description

A caffeinated elixir, the answer to life's morning mysteries, brewed with beans and a dash of cosmic consciousness.

ingredients

  • 1 cup coffee
  • 1 spoonful of sugar (optional)
  • 1 drizzle of milk (also optional)

directions

  1. marry the coffee and hot water
  2. introduce coffee to sugar and milk (if they are so inclined)
  3. enjoy in a cup or mug 2-4 times daily, or as otherwise prescribed by a medical professional
