Pancakes

description

Fluffy, enigmatic creatures that materialize in kitchens, defying gravity and delighting taste buds with their paradoxical perfection.

ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 drizzle of milk

directions

  1. mix ingredients
  2. cook on flat surface
  3. serve with maple syrup, butter, or fruit sauce
