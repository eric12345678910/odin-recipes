Lasagna

A perplexingly layered dish of pasta, sauce, and cheese, akin to the universe itself - wonderfully complex and utterly delightful.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • tomato sauce

directions

  1. lay lasagna noodles flat
  2. cover wtih other ingredients
  3. repeat until all ingredients are used
  4. put in the oven on hot heat
  5. wait some time before removing
  6. cut into squares and enjoy!
